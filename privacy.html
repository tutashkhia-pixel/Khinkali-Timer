Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Khinkali Dash, a productivity tool built by TechEdge Labs LLC ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Khinkali Dash operates primarily on your device. We do not use remote databases to store your personal tasks, timer history, or personal identifiers. We use local browser storage (localStorage/IndexedDB) to save your settings.

Google Calendar Integration

If you choose to use the Google Calendar Sync feature, our app requests read-only access to your calendar events (`calendar.readonly` scope). This data is fetched directly from Google's API to your browser. We do not transmit, read, or store your calendar data on any external servers.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use standard web storage technologies to save your local preferences, such as your selected language and custom feed URLs. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache.
